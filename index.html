Welcome to BigBrain Chess Academy

Located in the heart of Bahadurgarh, BigBrain Chess Academy is dedicated to spreading the love of chess and developing strategic thinking skills in players of all ages. Our academy combines traditional chess wisdom with modern teaching methods.

Whether you're a complete beginner taking your first steps on the 64 squares or an advanced player aiming for tournament success, our expert coaches provide personalized guidance to help you reach your full potential.
